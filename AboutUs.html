Who are we?

The Animal protection agency (APA) is a no profit organisation that strives to protect and save the earth and the many creatures that live on it ,the APA was established in 2024.


What do we do?

The APA works towards the protection of animals that are endangered and close to extinction ,we also work to ensure that animals in captivity are living in safe and sustainable environments where there is enough food and water and they are not being mistreated or harmed.The APA has also recently launched a initiative to help clean up the oceans and stop deforestation all over the world.

1.Donate to our to the APA your donation would go along way in ensuring that endangered species are well protected and that game reserves have the necessary man power to protect the majestic animals of the savanna from poaching.

3.you can do something as easy as sharing our cause on social media spreading awareness and informing more people on who we are and what we are about something as small as this can go along way in helping us helping the planet and helping the animals of this world.
